Our approach

Three things we don't compromise on

Fire

No gas, no electric

Everything that leaves our kitchen has touched the hearth — olive and oak wood burned down to embers, then cooked low and slow or blistered hot and fast.

Grove

Our own oil

We press our olive oil from a small grove two hours south. It finishes nearly everything on the menu, from the bread to the last course.

Coast

Fish, same-day

Whole fish comes in each morning from day boats. If the catch isn't right, it isn't on the board that night — no exceptions.
